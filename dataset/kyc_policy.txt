KYC & AML POLICY — CUSTOMER DUE DILIGENCE
Version 5.1 | Effective: 01-Apr-2024
Issuing Authority: Chief Compliance Officer

1. REGULATORY FRAMEWORK
This policy is governed by:
  - Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA) 2002
  - RBI Master Direction on KYC (2016, updated 2023)
  - FATF Recommendations
  - Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA) 1999

2. CUSTOMER IDENTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
2.1 Officially Valid Documents (OVDs) for Individuals:
  - Aadhaar (mandatory for resident Indians)
  - Passport
  - Driving Licence
  - Voter's Identity Card
  - NREGA Job Card
  - PAN Card (for financial transactions > ₹50,000)

2.2 Documents for Non-Individuals (Companies/Firms):
  - Certificate of Incorporation
  - Memorandum and Articles of Association
  - Board Resolution
  - PAN of entity and all directors/partners
  - Proof of registered office address

3. KYC CATEGORIES
3.1 Simplified KYC: Applicable for low-risk accounts with annual credit < ₹1L.
3.2 Full KYC: Mandatory for all other accounts. Requires OVD + photograph.
3.3 Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD): Mandatory for PEP, NRI, high-value, high-risk customers.

4. PERIODIC KYC REVIEW
4.1 High Risk customers: Every 2 years
4.2 Medium Risk customers: Every 8 years
4.3 Low Risk customers: Every 10 years
4.4 NRI accounts: Annual review mandatory
4.5 Dormant accounts: Full KYC on reactivation

5. VIDEO-BASED CUSTOMER IDENTIFICATION PROCESS (V-CIP)
5.1 Customer must be physically present for live video interaction.
5.2 Liveliness check mandatory — static image not accepted.
5.3 Facial match with OVD photograph to be conducted.
5.4 Agent must verify document details live on screen.
5.5 Session to be recorded and stored for 5 years.
5.6 Geolocation of customer to be captured during V-CIP.

6. CONSEQUENCES OF NON-COMPLIANCE
6.1 Account to be restricted: No credits/debits above ₹10,000.
6.2 Freeze after 6 months of pending KYC.
6.3 Account closure after 12 months if KYC not completed.
6.4 Regulatory reporting to Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU-IND).

7. CUSTOMER RIGHTS
7.1 Right to be informed of KYC requirements at account opening.
7.2 Right to update KYC information at any branch.
7.3 Right to appeal account freeze decision within 30 days.
7.4 Right to receive a copy of submitted documents.

8. STAFF OBLIGATIONS
8.1 Never photocopy OVDs without customer consent.
8.2 Mask Aadhaar number (last 4 digits only) in records.
8.3 Report suspicious customers to Compliance immediately.
8.4 Maintain customer confidentiality as per IT Act 2000.
