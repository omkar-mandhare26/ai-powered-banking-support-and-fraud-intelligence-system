REFUND & DISPUTE RESOLUTION POLICY
Version 2.3 | Effective: 15-Mar-2024
Issuing Authority: Head of Customer Experience

1. SCOPE
This policy covers disputes related to:
  - Failed transactions (amount debited, not credited to beneficiary)
  - Duplicate charges
  - Merchant disputes (goods/services not received)
  - ATM cash-not-dispensed complaints
  - UPI failure reversals

2. AUTO-REVERSAL TIMELINES (RBI Mandate)
2.1 UPI failed transactions: Auto-reversal within T+1 business day
2.2 IMPS failed transactions: Auto-reversal within T+1 business day
2.3 NEFT failed transactions: Auto-reversal within 2 hours on same day / next working day
2.4 Card declined transactions: Immediate (no amount deducted)
2.5 ATM cash-not-dispensed: T+5 working days auto-credit (₹100/day penalty if delayed)

3. MERCHANT DISPUTE PROCESS
3.1 Customer raises dispute within 30 days of transaction.
3.2 Bank raises chargeback with card network within 5 days.
3.3 Provisional credit within 10 working days (amounts > ₹1,000).
3.4 If merchant provides valid proof: Chargeback rejected, provisional credit reversed.
3.5 Final resolution within 45 working days.

4. REFUND MODES
4.1 Original payment mode preferred (card refund to card, UPI to source account).
4.2 NEFT if original mode unavailable.
4.3 Cheque in exceptional cases (account closed, etc.).
4.4 No cash refunds above ₹2,000.

5. ESCALATION MATRIX
5.1 Frontline Agent: Handle standard disputes (< ₹10,000, < 30 days old).
5.2 Senior Agent: Disputes ₹10,000–₹1,00,000 or > 30 days.
5.3 Nodal Officer: > ₹1,00,000 or unresolved after 15 days.
5.4 Grievance Redressal Officer: Legal notices, media complaints, ombudsman cases.

6. CUSTOMER COMPENSATION
6.1 If ATM delay > 5 days: ₹100 per day credited automatically.
6.2 If bank error causes loss: Full amount + 2% per annum interest from transaction date.
6.3 NEFT/RTGS wrong credit (bank error): Full refund within 2 working days.
6.4 NACH mandate wrong debit: Refund + ₹500 compensation per incident.
