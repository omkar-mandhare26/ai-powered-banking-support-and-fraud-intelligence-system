RETAIL LOAN PROCESSING POLICY
Version 4.0 | Effective: 01-Jan-2024
Issuing Authority: Head of Retail Credit

1. LOAN PRODUCT CATEGORIES
1.1 Home Loan: Up to ₹5 crore, tenure up to 30 years, LTV max 80%
1.2 Personal Loan: Up to ₹30L, tenure up to 5 years, unsecured
1.3 Car Loan: Up to ₹50L, tenure up to 7 years, vehicle as collateral
1.4 Education Loan: Up to ₹20L (domestic), ₹40L (abroad), moratorium during study
1.5 Business Loan: Up to ₹2 crore, secured/unsecured, tenure up to 7 years

2. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
2.1 Minimum age: 21 years; Maximum age at loan maturity: 65 years (salaried), 70 years (self-employed)
2.2 Minimum CIBIL Score:
  - Home Loan: 700
  - Personal Loan: 720
  - Car Loan: 680
  - Business Loan: 700
2.3 Minimum Income:
  - Salaried: ₹25,000/month net take-home
  - Self-employed: ₹3L annual net profit (ITR-based)
2.4 Employment stability: Minimum 2 years with current employer (salaried)

3. DOCUMENT CHECKLIST
3.1 Common documents (all loans):
  - KYC documents (Aadhaar + PAN)
  - Last 6-month bank statement
  - Last 3-month salary slips (salaried) / Last 2-year ITR (self-employed)
  - Loan application form

3.2 Additional (Home Loan):
  - Property documents, Title deed, Sale agreement
  - NOC from builder/society
  - Approved building plan

3.3 Additional (Business Loan):
  - GST returns (last 2 years)
  - Audited balance sheet
  - Business continuity proof (min 3 years)

4. PROCESSING TIMELINE
4.1 Personal Loan (pre-approved): Same day disbursement
4.2 Personal Loan (standard): 3-5 working days
4.3 Home Loan: 15-21 working days (subject to legal/technical verification)
4.4 Car Loan: 2-3 working days after delivery challan
4.5 Business Loan: 7-10 working days

5. INTEREST RATES (Indicative, subject to RBI policy)
5.1 Home Loan: 8.5% – 9.5% p.a. (floating, linked to RLLR)
5.2 Personal Loan: 10.5% – 18% p.a. (based on credit profile)
5.3 Car Loan: 8.75% – 11% p.a.
5.4 Education Loan: 8.5% – 12% p.a.
5.5 Business Loan: 11% – 16% p.a.

6. REJECTION REASONS & CUSTOMER COMMUNICATION
6.1 Low credit score: Advise customer to clear dues, avoid new credit, wait 6 months.
6.2 Insufficient income: Advise add co-applicant or opt for lower loan amount.
6.3 Document deficiency: Provide specific document list; customer can reapply within 30 days.
6.4 Negative credit history: Advise seek credit counselling; wait 12 months.
6.5 All rejection letters must be issued within 3 working days of decision.

7. FORECLOSURE & PART-PREPAYMENT
7.1 Floating rate loans: No foreclosure charges (as per RBI directive).
7.2 Fixed rate personal loans: 2% of outstanding principal.
7.3 Part-prepayment: Minimum ₹10,000; maximum 3 times per year.
7.4 No-dues certificate: Issued within 7 working days of final payment.

8. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL
8.1 Level 1: Branch manager — resolution within 5 working days.
8.2 Level 2: Nodal Officer — resolution within 15 working days.
8.3 Level 3: Banking Ombudsman — if unresolved within 30 days.
